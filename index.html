3.2 Language Focus: Stressed Words

Sentence Why we stress the bold word...
I love to read storybooks. Tells what kind of books
She has a red bicycle. Tells the color of the object

3.2.2 Voices Going Up (⤴) and Down (⤵)

Voice goes UP (⤴) for:

  • Greetings (Hello!)
  • Yes/No questions (Are you new here?)

Voice goes DOWN (⤵) for:

  • Statements (My name is Rina.)
  • Wh-questions (How are you?)
